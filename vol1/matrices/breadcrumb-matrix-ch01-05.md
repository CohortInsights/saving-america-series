## Notes

* Early chapters introduce multiple **seemingly unrelated clues**: burglaries, strange stolen items, and rumors about time travel.
* Chapter 3 connects these threads through the appearance of **Fox** and the threat to Paul Revere’s ride.
* Chapter 4 escalates the mystery by revealing the **underground scientific facility** and the claim that the teens themselves must carry out the mission.
* A new mystery emerges regarding **why children were chosen** rather than trained adult operatives.
